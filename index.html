Mission Statement

The Power of 2 will improve problem solving skills for k-12 students by providing low-cost professional development and materials to help parents, tutors and teachers integrate math and theater experiences in their instructional activities.

 

Vision Statement

Improve student achievement and interest in mathematics in the USA.
